# Melbourne Real Estate Data Analysis and Insights
**Objective**
Determine the top three regions driving real estate sales in Melbourne and evaluate monthly sales patterns to provide data-driven recommendations on regions for strategic expansion.

**Tableau Visualization and Analysis**
1.	**Data Preparation**
   
    o	Data is cleaned and transformed to ensure it is suitable for analysis and visualization.
  	
2.	**Visualization and Analysis**
   
The following charts are created to support analysis and derive insights:

    o	Pie Chart: Visualizes the percentage distribution of sales amount by region.
  	
    o	Side-by-Side Bar Chart: Compares housing types and sales amounts across the top three regions.
  	
    o	Trend Chart (Bar + Line): Displays sales trends over time, combining bar and line charts for better insight into patterns and growth.
  	
4.	**Summary and Insights**
   
    o	Key findings and actionable insights are summarized based on the visual analysis.
